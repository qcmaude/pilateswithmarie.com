Meet Marie

Marie has always been passionate about maintaining a healthy lifestyle – through food, exercise, and conversation. She has recently completed her instructor classes through Boston Body Works.

A resident of Plaistow since 2000, Marie has built lasting friendships in the community. She has been a client at Recesso since 2002 and a student of pilates at within the Recesso clinic since 2016.

She hopes you find the time to pop in and try her intimate class – whether you're a beginning or advanced student, she'll ensure you have a tough, safe, satisfying class.
