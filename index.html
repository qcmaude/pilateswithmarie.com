Meet Marie

Marie has always been passionate about maintaining a healthy lifestyle: from step aerobics to yoga, she's tried it all! She combines her extensive exercise experience with her warm, attentive personality to create a unique, tailored pilates experience.

She is currently working to obtain her instructor certification through Balanced Body taught at Boston Body and teaching at Recesso Physical Therapy in Plaistow, NH. See her schedule below.

A resident of Plaistow since 2000, Marie has built lasting friendships in the community. She has been a physical therapy client at Recesso since 2002 and a student of pilates within the Recesso clinic since early 2018.
